About Blue Link Mining and Logistics

Blue Link Mining and Logistics (Pty) Ltd is a South African logistics company focused on the transportation of coal and other minerals. The company supports mining, industrial and commercial clients by providing reliable transport solutions designed around safety, efficiency and dependable service delivery.

Under the leadership of Luyanda Chiliza, Blue Link is committed to building long-term relationships with clients by delivering professional logistics support, responsive communication and consistent operational performance.

Whether transporting coal, minerals or other bulk materials, Blue Link Mining and Logistics aims to be a trusted logistics partner for businesses operating in the mining and resources sector.
